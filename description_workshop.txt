[h1]Munchies All-Calamity Addon[/h1]

 You need [url=https://steamcommunity.com/sharedfiles/filedetails/?id=3310041861]Munchies mod[/url] to run this mod. 

This mod works as a permanent power-ups tracker for most of the Calamity related. Right now, the mods can track the following consumables.
